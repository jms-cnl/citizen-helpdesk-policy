Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 30, 2026

Citizen Helpdesk is a capstone project that enables citizens to submit complaints through Facebook Messenger and SMS.

Information We Collect

Purpose

The collected information is used only for receiving, organizing, categorizing, and routing complaints to the appropriate government department.

Data Sharing

Citizen Helpdesk does not sell or share personal information with third parties.
